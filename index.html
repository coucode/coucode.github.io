About Me

I love solving complex problems and making things more efficient.

Throughout my early career, I gravitated toward roles in Operations Management, Quality Assurance, and Learning and Development that allowed me to solve complex operational challenges and improve operational efficiency. While these opportunities were exciting and insightful, I wanted more.

I have always had an interest in software development, but this interest blossomed when I learned SQL for the first time. I was obsessed with putting together millions of seemingly separate pieces of data to provide valuable insights. I wanted to build the skills to take that obsession to the next level and build software that provides value.

As a full-stack software developer, I am thankful for the opportunity to consistently solve complex problems and leverage my background and expertise to build software that provides value and is efficient for its users. I have enjoyed using JavaScript, Python, React/Redux, HTML, CSS, and Git for my projects.
